About Me

Hi! I'm Amogh, welcome to my website. You can expect to find information about my technical prowess and work-experience, which should help you understand me and my skills better. 

I love problem solving. I learned how to solve a Rubik's cube when I was in school and the sense of accomplishment I got from it was unparalleled. The feeling of accomplishment encapsulated with my want to solve more convoluted problems got me into endeavors like Chess, where I mostly excelled when I wasn't rage quitting. A life changing moment occurred when I was introduced to programming with C++ in 11th grade. Solving simple problems like finding whether a string is a palindrome imbibed in me a sense of the vast amount of possibilities that the world of programming encompasses. Recognising my ever-existing love for solving problems, I decided to pursue Computer Science Engineering for both my undergraduate and graduate studies. With a formal education in the field, I hoped to obtain mastery of the best technologies and use it to build impactful, fun projects. While I am doing more Leetcode than I would've liked, I believe that I have made good progress towards my goal and am looking to do even better in the future. 

Outside of my love for problem-solving, I am extremely passionate about Sports and Wildlife. I mostly only watch sports as opposed to playing, since it feels futile to play it myself when I can watch more accomplished individuals perform magic on the field. Watching Livepool play football can make or break my whole day depending on the result of the game. 
As for Wildlife, I am simply following in on my dad's footsteps, who would switch to the Discovery channel at dinner and ensured that we ate with ferocious carnivores. I am highly intrigued by snakes and spiders, with my current obsession extending to the weird creatures that exist in the depths of the ocean.
